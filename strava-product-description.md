## Domain / Category

Health & Fitness app (official App Store category). Functionally a hybrid: GPS activity tracker + social network + competitive layer (segments, challenges).

## Target Users

Verified via WebSearch on 2026-07-01, sourced from Strava's Year in Sport Trend Report and Strava Business audience page:

- **Running remains the top tracked sport**, with racing on the rise.
- **Walking is the #2 most-recorded activity type** — the platform's audience is broader than "elite athletes."
- **54% of users track multiple activity types**; **96% participate in multiple sports**.
- **93% cite health as their primary motivator** for being active (not just performance/competition).
- The platform has shifted from its "elite cyclists and ultramarathoners" image to a much broader general-fitness audience.
- More than 40% of new athletes joining in 2024 were women — the highest share reported.
- Gen Z is a growing segment, driving community/club growth.

**Implication for this report:** Strava is not just a tool for competitive runners/cyclists — a first-time, casual user (like the walk recorded for this project) is representative of a large and growing share of the real user base, not an edge case.
