Finding 1 · Why & Fix

The task never reached “closure”

ExpectedFinish = done → show me my activity
Reality4 more screens, ending in a forced Share prompt
HCIBreaks “design dialogs to yield closure” & user control
CauseThe flow doesn't end where the mental model says it should
Mental modelGolden Rule #4 — closureGolden Rule #7 — control
One closure screen: “Saved. View now · Share later”
Haptic buzz on Start / Pause / Finish — eyes stay on the road
Give the task a clear ending the user controls.

CSC13112 · PA109 · Finding 4
Synthesis

One pattern behind all four findings

Core loop

Speaks the beginner's language.
Familiar actions, familiar icons, clear steps.

Everything around it

Assumes you already know Strava.
Jargon · hidden navigation · no closure.

Strava onboards the action — not the vocabulary.
CSC13112 · PA110 · Pattern
Solutions

Three cheap moves, all realistic

  1. Close & confirm
    End tasks clearly — a closure screen + haptic feedback (Finding 1)
  2. Plain language, in place
    Define jargon where it appears — info icon, one-line subtitle (Findings 2 & 3)
  3. One job per tap
    Distinct affordances — separate “view” from “act” (Finding 4)
Small in-context cues turn trial-and-error into recognition.
